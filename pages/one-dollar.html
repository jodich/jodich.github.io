$1

As Singapore advances towards a more developed country, it is inevitable that the cost of living continues to increase. The utilization of the Singapore $1 coin will be employed as a representation within this project. The $1 coins were renewed respectively in 1967, 1987 and 2013, and these selected years would be used to show the difference in quantity one can buy with that amount during these varying economic circumstances.

This publication hopes to heighten the awareness and seriousness of the rising cost of living in Singapore.

The estimated equivalent one can purchase with $1 in 2013 to 1967 and 1987 are calculated using Monetary Authority of Singapore’s Goods & Services Inflation Calculator.
