PiSS

We allow ourselves to become the subject of surveillance in order to feel safe. However, the excessive security measures we implement are actually making ourselves and our information more vulnerable. For this project, a satirical surveillance system is created to mock our excessive need for such vigilance. Introducing PiSS (Personal Intelligence Surveillance System) a security system that repurposes existing surveillance methods to protect an individual’s intimate and personal safety, a mobile app accompanies the system.

Try out the app here and for more detailed explanation on how the app works, download the PDF here.

PiSS’s advertisement uses “cult-like” approaches to establish its omnipresence and omniscience. The message on the poster resembles messages from an “higher authority” to establish the brand’s power and dependability. The posters are interactive as cloud-like texture gently move in the background to produce a serene ambience. With further emphasize on the brand’s abilities, a disturbance is created in the cloud-like background when an audience walks past the advertisement.
