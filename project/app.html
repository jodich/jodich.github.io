Project
TRANSFORMING E-COMMERCE FOR MOBILE
Role: Research, Design
Status: On Hold
Objective

With the majority of our users shopping through their mobile phones, our brand aimed to leverage mobile device features and functionalities to offer an enhanced shopping experience.

✨New Features for an Enhanced Shopping Experience✨
AR Try-On

Utilising the app's access to the mobile device's camera, the AR Try-On feature allows users to virtually try on products such as shoes and sunglasses. This interactive feature makes shopping more engaging and fun.

Image Search

The image search feature, leveraging easy access to the camera and photo gallery, enables users to snap and upload images from their gallery to find similar products. This offers a quick and convenient way for users to discover items they like while on the go.

Membership ID

With the app always accessible on their mobile devices, users can enhance their offline experience as well. Easy access to membership ID and features allows users to retrieve their information during in-store purchases, ensuring that their transactions and details are connected to their online accounts.

Push Notifications

The app's push notifications keep users informed about their orders, latest promotions, new arrivals, and even personalised content. This feature helps encourage repeat visits and reminds users of our brand's presence

A Streamlined User Interface

In addition to introducing new features, we optimised the app's user interface by removing unnecessary elements and content meant solely for SEO purposes. We also refined gestures and scroll behaviours to ensure seamless browsing.

Dedicated Design System

We developed a dedicated design system to ensure consistency and visual harmony, adhering to best practices for mobile apps. This system unifies our brand's identity with the app's functionality, delivering a cohesive and intuitive user experience.

✨Conclusion✨

With these enhancements, we have successfully leveraged the unique capabilities of mobile devices to create a more engaging and user-friendly shopping experience. These improvements not only enhance our users' online and offline interactions with the brand but also help maintain a strong, consistent brand identity. By prioritizing user needs and optimizing functionality, we have laid a foundation for continued growth in user satisfaction and engagement.
