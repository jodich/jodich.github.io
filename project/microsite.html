FALL WINTER 2021 MICROSITE
Skill: Front-end Development
Status: Launched
Project Overview

In the 2021 autumn winter campaign for Charles & Keith, I created an interactive microsite that would drive user engagement and enhance visibility. This microsite had features of dynamic scroll animations which provided an immersive experience aligning with the visual and functional needs of the campaign.

My Role

As Front-end Developer, my main job was to ensure that the microsite was visually appealing and easily accessible on any device. To achieve desired interactive effects and smooth performance, I used HTML, CSS, jQuery as well as a useful plugin.

Development Process

I managed my time efficiently to meet this campaign's tight deadline. The first step in doing this is always thorough research which helped me to estimate how much effort it will take me to develop and plan my project timeline accordingly. Working closely with the art director from our brand, I harmoniously incorporated the campaign's visuals into a microsite with them.

Reflections

This project showed how important strategic planning is for effective collaboration. Despite the restricted time frame, I produced a high quality interactive website that enhanced user participation while successfully reflecting all aims of the campaign involved.
