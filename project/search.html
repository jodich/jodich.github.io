SEARCH FEATURE FOR E-COMMERCE
Role: Research, Design
Status: Development
Importance of Search Functionality

The search function on e-commerce websites is an important component for enhancing user experience and conversion. By efficiently matching user queries with the most relevant results, it significantly accelerates the browsing process. Our heuristic findings revealed that 43% of site visitors immediately use the search function upon arrival, a statistic supported by our site's heatmap data, highlighting its high usage.

Users who engage with the search feature demonstrates a higher likelihood of making purchases. This is because they often have a clear intent to find specific products, indicating a strong buying intention.

Recognizing the crucial role of the search feature in user satisfaction and sales performance, our team has undertaken an audit and redesign of the feature. This initiative aims to optimize the search functionality, ensuring it meets its full potential in facilitating user navigation and boosting conversion.

✨Research✨

Logic:Utilizing Baymard's search audit methodology, I conducted a thorough audit of our search function. This involved testing various popular search queries, extracted from Google Analytics, to assess the accuracy and relevancy of the search outcomes. The audit revealed several gaps, particularly in product prioritization, and an overemphasis on editorial content. This indicated a need for refining our search algorithm to better prioritise products over other content types.

Interface:Based on UX research and competitor analysis, I concluded that our search interface required improvements in clarity and functionality. This redesign focused on retaining essential elements that aid the search process, while optimizing space allocation to ensure a user experience that effectively fulfills search intent. This involved simplifying the interface and enhancing the quality of search results.

Business Alignment:In addition to addressing logic and design considerations, I aimed to align our search function with strategic business goals. This included emphasizing our flagship products, thematic collections, and editorial content. By promoting these items within search results, we can reinforce our brand identity and provide greater value to our users. This alignment not only enhances the user experience but also supports our broader business objectives by driving engagement with key product offerings and curated content.

✨Solution✨
Maximizing usability and visibility

Enhanced Search Bar
To encourage users to search, we increased the prominence of the search touchpoint on the header. By prioritizing essential elements and removing or repositioning those that were minimally used, we created more negative space, thereby emphasizing the search bar. This redesign makes the search function more noticeable and accessible, inviting users to engage with it immediately upon visiting the site.

Dynamic Search Menu
Before users even start typing, we offer tailored suggestions to anticipate and fulfill their interests. This dynamic menu includes popular search queries, personalised product recommendations, and dynamic search suggestions, streamlining the search process and helping users find what they're looking for more quickly.

  • Popular Search Queries: This feature allows users to quickly discover popular items and gain insights into current trends. By showcasing what other users are searching for, we create a sense of urgency and curiosity (or also known as FOMO), encouraging users to explore these trending items. This not only aids in easy navigation but also enhances user engagement by tapping into social proof.
  • Personalised Product Recommendations: Showcasing a curated set of products based on the user's browsing history, this feature expedites navigation by anticipating user interests. By presenting products that align with their browsing behavior, we provide a more personalised shopping experience, increasing the likelihood of conversion.
  • Dynamic Search Suggestions: The placeholder text in the search bar changes dynamically with each search menu opening, highlighting popular categories, trends, and themes. This feature prompts users to explore products or content they may not have initially considered, broadening their engagement with the site and potentially leading to increased sales.
User-Centric Features

Smart Suggestions
Upon initiating a search, users receive relevant suggestions based on a sophisticated algorithm that has been rigorously tested and iterated upon. This ensures that the suggestions are accurate, timely, and aligned with the user's intent, enhancing the overall search experience.

  • Keyword Suggestions: This feature auto-completes the user's query, allowing them to navigate their search more efficiently. By predicting and displaying potential search terms, users can quickly select their desired query, reducing the time and effort required to find specific products or information.
  • Product Recommendations: Displays the most relevant products based on the user's query. Given the limited number of products shown in the initial search results, a "View All" prompt is included as the last thumbnail. This prompt directs users to the comprehensive search results page, encouraging them to continue browsing and use filters to further refine their search.
  • Editorials: Our brand produces editorial content that includes style guides and fashion trends. Integrating this content into the search results provides an opportunity to showcase information that aligns with our user's interests. This enriches their shopping experience by offering valuable information, tips, or insights, thereby enhancing user engagement and satisfaction.
Continuous Browsing

Error Handling
During the audit, it was observed that typos or queries with no results caused the search menu to stop displaying suggestions, even when the system provided a “did you mean …” suggestion. To mitigate user frustration and improve their experience, we implemented the following changes:

  • Instant Corrections: When a typo is detected, the search suggestions instantly update based on the corrected spelling, ensuring users receive relevant results without additional steps.
  • Fallback Suggestions: If no results are found, the system will default to displaying popular or relevant suggestions, ensuring users are continuously presented with opportunities to explore and discover our products.
Maintenance

Maintaining an effective search feature requires ongoing effort due to the dynamic nature of our brand, characterized by weekly releases and evolving user interests. Key maintenance activities include:

Regular Updates
  • Search Index Updates: Regularly updating the search index to include new products and remove discontinued ones ensures that the search results are current and accurate.
  • Algorithm Fine-Tuning: Continuously monitoring and fine-tuning the search algorithm is necessary to adapt to changing user behaviors and preferences, improving the relevancy of search results.

Regular Audits
  • Identification and Resolution: Conducting regular audits helps identify and address issues promptly. This proactive approach ensures that the search function remains effective and efficient.
  • User Feedback Incorporation: Actively incorporating user feedback and insights into the search optimization process will help ensure that the search feature consistently provides reliable and relevant information, enhancing the overall user experience.

✨Conclusion✨

The overhaul of our search functionality significantly enhances user satisfaction and engagement by prioritizing usability, visibility, and user-centric features like smart suggestions and continuous browsing. Ongoing maintenance, including regular updates, algorithm fine-tuning, and user feedback incorporation, ensures the search feature remains relevant and reliable. These improvements aim to strengthen our brand's connection with customers, fostering trust and positioning our platform as a leader in exceptional e-commerce solutions.
